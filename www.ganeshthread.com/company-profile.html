About Shree Ganesh Jari Covering

Ganesh Group of Companies is a leading manufacturer of Flora Zari (30D,70D,100D) , Polyester Jari (150D,180D), Nylon Jari (110D),Half Fine Zari, Imitation Zari, Embroidery Thread, Metallic Film, Mettalic Rolls, Mx Jari, Polyester Yarn and Viscose Yarn. Established in 2008, we have achieved several milestones. Ganesh Jari and Thread is known to provide its superior quality products and utmost customer satisfaction service.

With our presence in 8 States of India and 2 Countries, today we are actively working with 350 dealers and proudly serving more than 1500 customers. We are fully equipped to fulfill Quality, Quantity and On Time delivery. We have capacity of manufacturing 200 Ton Flora Zari and 100 Ton Polyester yarn per month.

"Perfect Length" is the Moto of our company and we go the extra mile to achieve this. We work to transform the world of Textile Industry with Technology and to become a globally recognized name.

We have developed a sophisticated infrastructure facility at our premises. To manufacture our offered collection of products as per the industry defined norms and standards, our facility is equipped with all the modern machines and tools. To control all our business process, we have appointed a team of well qualified and experienced professionals. With the constant support and dedicated efforts of our team members, we have been able to achieve our predefined objectives.
